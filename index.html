Yoghurt Project

Yogurt is a fermented milk product and as such is a means of preserving the nutrients in milk. A wide variety of yogurts are now available around the world, ranging from very-low-fat fruit yogurts to Greek-style yogurt with a fat content around 8 g per 100 g. Yogurt can be made from cows', ewes', goats', or buffalo's milk. Since yogurt is derived from milk, it provides protein, calcium, and other minerals, and a range of vitamins.
